About Department of Mathmatics

The SUSTech Mathematics Department was founded in June 2015. As of the 2022 - 2023 academic year, the Department consists of 42 research and teaching-line faculty members and 10 teaching-line faculty members, 267 graduate students, and 146 undergraduate majors. The Department is currently located in the College of Science Building, 1088 Xueyuan Avenue. Our administrative office is situated in Room M715 on the seventh floor.

Our graduate program (master and doctoral) covers nearly all fields of mathematical sciences. In addition, the Department also has joint Ph.D. programs with a few well-known overseas and Hong Kong/Macao universities providing excellent opportunities for prospective students.

Our faculty members’ research covers a broad range of areas including Mathematics (dynamical systems, algebra and combinatorics, algebraic number theory, PDEs, mathematical physics and differential geometry, etc.), Computational and Applied Mathematics (applied mathematics, numerical analysis, computational fluid dynamics, scientific computing, inverse problems, data science, etc.), Probability and Statistics, and Financial Mathematics.

About Center

The Jermanov Mathematics Center at Southern University of Science and Technology is a Shenzhen Nobel Laureate Scientist Laboratory, funded by the Shenzhen Municipal Government and led by SUSTech. It aims to enhance and lead scientific research in fundamental mathematics, applied mathematics, and computational mathematics in Shenzhen, the Greater Bay Area, and the broader southern China region.

The center focuses on crucial research areas in pure and applied mathematics, dedicated to advancing foundational mathematical theories in China and fostering interdisciplinary research and applications that intersect with life sciences, information technology, engineering, and finance. It strives to address significant scientific challenges and meet major national and regional needs.

Operating to international standards and rules, the Mathematics Center creates first-class academic conditions and environments. Through specialized academic year programs and innovative research team activities, it aims to establish itself as a world-class international platform for mathematical research and exchange, as well as a premier venue for talent attraction and development. The center is committed to building a globally leading research team and substantially elevating the level of mathematical research and education.
